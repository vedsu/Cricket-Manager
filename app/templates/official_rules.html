Core Gameplay Rules

Gameplay fundamentals
1
Bowling Action

Strictly under-arm, released below waist level. Any over-arm or side-arm is a No-Ball.

2
The Bounce

Ball must bounce at least once before the popping crease. Full-toss above knee is a No-Ball.

3
The "Six-is-Out" Rule (USP)

If the ball clears the boundary on the full, the batsman is OUT.

If it bounces and goes over, it is 4 Runs.

4
No-Bounce Zone

The first bounce must occur in the middle or second half of the pitch. Skipping the bounce in the initial safety zone is a Dead Ball.

5
Dismissals
Bowled Caught Run Out Stumped Six-Out

No LBW

6
Extras

Wide Ball and No-Ball grant 1 Run plus extra delivery. No Free Hits or Powerplays.
